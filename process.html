How we work

A clear process, from idea to launch

No black boxes and no radio silence for three weeks. Here's exactly what happens at each stage, and what you can expect from us along the way.

01

Discover

Before anything gets designed or coded, we need to understand what you're actually trying to achieve: who the audience is, what's worked or failed before, and what success looks like six months from now.

  • Kickoff conversation covering goals, audience, and constraints
  • Review of any existing brand assets, sites, or apps
  • Scope and timeline laid out in plain language before work starts
02

Design

Concepts and direction come first (wireframes for products, mood boards and drafts for identity work), so we agree on the direction before time is spent refining details that might change.

  • Initial concepts presented with reasoning, not just options
  • Structured feedback rounds, refined until it's right
  • Final visual direction signed off before development begins
03

Develop

Approved designs get built out with clean, tested code: a website, an app, or finished brand assets ready for production. Progress is visible throughout, not revealed all at once at the end.

  • Regular progress check-ins, not a single reveal at the finish line
  • Testing across devices and browsers before anything ships
  • Staging links or previews so you can see real progress
04

Deliver

Launch day is a milestone, not the end of the relationship. Files, source code, and guidelines are handed over properly, and support continues after the fact for fixes and small updates.

  • Full handover of source files, assets, and documentation
  • Launch support to handle anything that comes up in the first days
  • Ongoing support available for updates and future work
